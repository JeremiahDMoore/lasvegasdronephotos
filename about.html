Aerial Video, Photo, and Editing Services 







Aces Up Media is a drone photography company located in Las Vegas, Nevada. We specialize in aerial photography and 4k drone videos for real estate brokers, realtors, and general construction firms. We have a curated collection of 4k drone stock footage so you can purchase the clips you need for creating your own content.

We specialize in providing drone services for Las Vegas and surrounding areas. Our photos help home buyers see what their investment will look like. We take aerial photos of construction sites, houses, commercial real estate and more! Check out our portfolio for some of the great drone photos we have taken.

Aerial photography is an excellent tool to take photos of your next real estate listing to show your remote clients. We can empower you with the ability to easily showcase your project through interactive virtual tours, aerial video and photography.

We have over 5 years experience in the drone industry and will bring you unique, one-of-a-kind footage at prices you can afford. If you would like to learn more about our company or have any questions about anything we do, just give us a call or send us an email.
