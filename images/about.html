About Us 







👋 Hello!


Our mission is to provide you with quality photography and videography services, helping you to achieve your project goals. We guarantee 100% satisfaction to all of our clients. We are a group of experienced, Zillow certified photographers and videographers based in Las Vegas, NV, USA. Photographing Las Vegas real estate, Filming and editing marketing videos and providing excellent service is what Aces Up Media is all about!

Aces Up Media is much more than another Las Vegas drone company. In addition to professional drone photography services, we offer many other photo and video services. Event filming, business marketing videos, music video shoots, construcion photography, and video editing. We also have a collection of drone stock footage, including aerial video of the Las Vegas strip.

Las Vegas realtors use our photography and videography services to boost their sales, and market luxury property for sale. Drone video for real estate marketing is our most popular service right now. FAA certified drone pilots for hire can get authorization to fly drones in restricted areas. Safety is our top priority! We care about your people, pets, and property! Liability insured up to $10 million (depending on the job).

Aerial photography for inspection is also something we are good at. Certified part 107 drone operators inspect your roof, solar panels, or cellular towers, keeping your people safe on the ground.
